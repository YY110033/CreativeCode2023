Project #7 Arrays & Data Portrait



Data Visualization: The number of questions I asked and I received during five days.

Sometimes I felt impatient with people's questions. After this self-tracking, I realized that these questions (whether I ask them or I receive them) always contain care and love, or spark some interesting conversations. So I made them in different lovely colors.





Step 1: Sketch

At first, I made a text version. Trying to understand how to put content into an empty array and then present the content by using for loops.






Step 2: Data Visualization

After understanding how it works, I tried to transfer text content into visual language. I use different colors to represent different days, and use the directions of lines to show if these questions was I asked or I received.







Objective

Create a data portrait out of a personal habit of yours.

Description

For this project, you will spend a 'week' tracking a personal habit of yours to create a data-self portrait. Choose the personal habit mindfully — you will need to collect 20 ~ 50 data points for this assignment. Record the data onto a numbered list every time it happens, and translate the list into array in p5.js. Animate your data self-portrait using any of the programming concepts you’ve learned in this class. Include an instruction on your CC portfolio to teach your audience how to read your data self-portrait.
